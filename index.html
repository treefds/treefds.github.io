A Wizard's Spring Hike
A Wizard's Spring Hike

[Play Online!] [Github Repo]

Made in 72 hours for Ludum Dare 55 "Summoning" (April 2024). Developed independently with Godot 4, featuring original game designs, art & code.

In this Sokoban-inspired puzzle game, the player controls the Wizard to put together materials and summon golems to push obstacles, press switches and navigate through the garden labyrinth.

Scrapside Picnic
Scrapside Picnic

[Play Online!]

Made in 72 hours for Ludum Dare 56 "Tiny Creatures" (Oct 2024). Developed independently with Godot 4, featuring original game designs, art & code.

A 2D exploration-puzzle platformer game about very-tiny alien visitors stranded on planet Earth. Explore an out-of-scale picnic site, where soda cans contain lethal liquid and sparrows become giant monsters.

The gameplay is influenced by the Metroidvania genre, while the narrative is a twist on the science fiction novel Roadside Picnic.

Hearthfront
Hearthfront

Course project for CS1119-01 "Tencent Game Program Design and Case Study" (Sept–Dec 2024). Co-developed with Zeyi Zheng and Zhe Su. I worked as the main designer, programmer and artist.

A precise puzzle game featuring two core game mechanics: Time Rewind and Ghost Possession. (Think Braid and Ghost Trick: Phantom Detective, played out in a Baba Is You fashion!) The player controls a ghost to move between and manipulate objects through the timeline to progress the game.

The game is created as a course project and is unpublished as of yet.
